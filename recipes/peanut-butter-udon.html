Peanut Butter Udon

This Peanut Butter Udon Noodles recipe is a ridiculously easy and delicious meal that you can whip up in less than 5 minutes – without any cooking (which is also perfect for anyone living in a dorm or any day when you don’t feel like cooking over the stove)!

Ingredients

  • Udon noodles
  • Peanut Butter
  • Maple syrup
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Frozen vegetables

Steps

  1. Add udon noodles and frozen veggies to a microwave-safe bowl filled with hot water. No need to measure as you'll be draining it afterwards. Microwave for 2 minutes.
  2. While microwaving, make the peanut butter sauce by mixing together peanut butter and maple syrup. Then add in apple cider vinegar and soy sauce. Mix until smooth.
  3. Drain the water once you've microwaved your udon noodles and vegetables.
  4. Pour sauce into the noodles and mix well.
  5. Top with crushed red peppers and toasted sesame seeds (optional). Enjoy!
